Figure 2. Correlation matrix of continuous predictors in Dataset A.

From the correlation plot, it seems that lymph_nodes and tumor_size has a moderate positive correlation r = 0.71 , which makes sense in a clinical context. Everything else seems largely uncorrelated; there is no severe multicollinearity, OLS shouldn’t be too unstable. The mild correlation between lymph_nodes and tumor_size may result in one predictor being dropped by LASSO, so elastic net might be preferred here.
